Our history

The founder of the group is Mr. Lorenzini Guido, with over 40 years of experience in construction, who wanted from the beginning for the activity to be a family business, he being fully assisted by his son Lorenzini Davide, representing the second generation of entrepreneurs of the Lorenzini family, being actively involved in the administration of the group's affairs.


15 years of successful activity are just some of the results of our group. This abundant portfolio is the result of a valuable mission argued in favor of our organization through principles and ethics together with professional competence, a competent management team as well as the executive team, serious partners and collaborators, both from Romania and outside the country, with whom We have built a reputation that can guarantee the good execution of all our projects.
